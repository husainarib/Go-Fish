How to play Go Fish!

Setup:

  • 2 to 6 players can play.
  • Deal 5 cards to each player (or 7 cards if only 2 or 3 players).
  • The rest of the cards go in a pile in the middle of the table.

Goal:

  • Your goal is to collect 4 cards of the same number (like four 3s or four Kings).

How to Play:

  • On your turn, ask any other player for a card by saying something like, "Do you have any 5s?"
  • If they have the card you asked for, they give you all of those cards.
  • If they don’t have it, they’ll say, “Go Fish!” and you’ll pick a card from the pile in the middle.
  • If you pick the card you asked for, you get to go again. If not, it's the next player's turn.

Making a Set:

  • If you collect all 4 cards of the same number (like four 7s), put them face-up on the table. That’s called a "book."

Winning:

  • The game ends when all the cards are used up or all the books are made.
  • The player with the most books at the end is the winner!
